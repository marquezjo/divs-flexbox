My Sphynx Cats

I currently own two Canadian Sphynx cats named Ickis and Mew. My obsession with Sphynx cats began in 2012 when I first encountered the breed. I got my first Sphynx, Ickis, in December 2019 from a breeder in San Diego. However, Ickis would get lonely when I left for work, so I brought Mew home less than two months after bringing Ickis home.

Ickis is my male sphynx. He always has the meanest look on his face. He's my mini gargoyle. Ickis was named after a character from Nickelodeon's "Ahhhhh! Real Monsters"
Then there's Mew, my female Sphynx, and as you may have guessed. I am a Pokemon fan! Mew got her name from the cute psychic Pokemon that shares her name. And, yes, she really does make a 'mew' sound.
Sphynx cats have earned the nickname 'Velcro Cats' because they tend to stick to their owners or each other. Despite their perpetually grumpy appearance, these two are actually very sweet-natured.
